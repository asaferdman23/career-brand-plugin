How it works

/brand

Personal Branding Engine

Give it a raw idea, a thing you built, or a moment from your week. It asks 2-3 sharp questions, reads your git log for authentic details, then drafts a post in your storyteller voice with multiple hook options. It also plans monthly content calendars aligned with your career goals.

create a post plan my month brainstorm ideas track performance
/career

Career Co-Pilot

Strategic career advice that accounts for your full picture — job search, side project, startup, unique background. Evaluates opportunities, optimizes your LinkedIn profile, preps you for interviews with stories from YOUR life, and builds networking strategies that actually work.

career direction job search networking interview prep

Under The Hood

Built for developers,
not influencers

✎

Storyteller Voice

Trained to write like a person, not a brand. Leads with tension, ends with insight. Never says "excited to share."

🛠

Git-Aware Content

Reads your recent commits for build-in-public posts. Authentic technical details, not made-up buzzwords.

🎯

Career-Aligned

Every post serves your career goals. A career-advisor agent ensures your content strategy matches your direction.

🌍

Multilingual

Posts in any language. Suggests the right one based on topic and audience. Local stories local, tech leadership global.

👥

Dual-Path Aware

Have a startup AND a job search? Every recommendation accounts for both paths without sacrificing either.

📈

Performance Memory

Tell it how posts performed. It learns what works for YOUR audience and adjusts future content accordingly.
