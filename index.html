The solution

A plugin that knows what you actually built

Career Brand isn't another AI writer. It's a Claude Code plugin that understands your work, your story, and your goals — then helps you tell it.

Context-aware Reads your real work

It reads your career history, git commits, and project context. Posts are grounded in what you actually built — not hallucinated fluff.

Your voice Storyteller, not corporate

Gets smarter Remembers what works

Saves your profile, tracks post performance, remembers your goals. Every post gets better because it knows your arc.

How it works

Build something. Run one command. Post.

1

Install the plugin

Two commands inside Claude Code. Takes 30 seconds.

/plugin marketplace add asaferdman23/career-brand-plugin
/plugin install career-brand@career-brand
2

Paste your experience

Copy your LinkedIn about section, resume, or just type what you do. The plugin builds your career profile in 60 seconds.

3

Tell it what you built

Give it a raw idea — a feature you shipped, a bug you solved, a workflow you automated. It asks 2-3 shaping questions.

> /career-brand:brand I automated our entire deploy pipeline with Claude
4

Pick a hook. Post.

It gives you 3 hook options. You pick one. Get a polished post in your voice. Copy. Paste to LinkedIn. Done.
