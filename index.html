How to set up Career Brand

No account needed. No signup. Just your terminal and your LinkedIn URL.

1

Install Claude Code

Claude Code is Anthropic's free AI agent that runs in your terminal. If you already have it, skip to step 2.

$ npm install -g @anthropic-ai/claude-code
Requires Node.js 18+. Takes about 30 seconds.
$ npm install -g @anthropic-ai/claude-code
added 1 package in 12s
$ claude
Welcome to Claude Code v2.1...
✓ Ready
2

Add the Career Brand plugin

One command adds the plugin to your Claude Code. It's free, open source, and works immediately.

$ claude plugin add github:asaferdman23/career-brand-plugin
This gives you two new commands: /brand and /career
$ claude plugin add github:asaferdman23/career-brand-plugin
Downloading career-brand-plugin...
✓ Plugin installed
/brand — Personal branding
/career — Career co-pilot
3

Run /brand and paste your LinkedIn

The AI scans your entire LinkedIn profile — experience, skills, posts, engagement patterns. Builds your career profile in 90 seconds. Then you're ready to create content.

$ /brand
It will ask for your LinkedIn URL, then 3 quick follow-up questions.
> /brand
What's your LinkedIn URL?
> linkedin.com/in/yourname
Scanning your profile...
Reading experience, skills, posts...
✓ Profile loaded!
Your Brand Snapshot:
Strengths: Strong technical depth
Gap: No storytelling in posts
Quick win: Your career change story
4

Create your first post

Give it any raw idea — something you built, learned, or experienced. It asks a couple of shaping questions, then drafts a post with multiple hook options. Pick one, copy, paste to LinkedIn. Done.

$ /brand I just led a product launch that doubled activation
You can also type /brand plan my month or /brand ideas
> /brand I led a launch that doubled activation
What almost went wrong?
> We almost killed it. Data looked terrible.
Hook A: "We almost killed our best feature."
Hook B: "The metric that saved our launch wasn't in any dashboard."
Hook C: "Two weeks before launch, I had to convince my VP not to pull the plug."
