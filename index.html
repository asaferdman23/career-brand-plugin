Plugin for Claude Code

Turn your career into LinkedIn posts that actually sound like you

Paste your experience. Tell it a raw idea. Get a post with your real voice — not "I'm thrilled to announce" garbage. Also: career advice, interview prep, and monthly content planning.

Install in Claude Code
$ claude plugin install asaferdman23/career-brand-plugin

Requires Claude Code (Anthropic's CLI agent). Free plugin, open source, MIT licensed.

Two commands. That's the whole plugin.

/career-brand:brand Personal branding

Create LinkedIn posts from a raw idea. It asks shaping questions, gives you hook options, drafts in a storyteller voice. Also: plan your month, brainstorm ideas, set post reminders.

/career-brand:career Career co-pilot

Career direction, job search strategy, networking playbook, interview prep with mock questions. If you have a side project, it accounts for both paths.

Paste your profile, not your password

Copy-paste your LinkedIn about section, resume, or just type your experience. No browser access needed, no login required. Works for yourself or for someone else.

Remembers your story

Saves your career profile, goals, content calendar, and what posts performed well. Every post gets better because it knows your arc, your voice, your goals.

Setup in 2 minutes

1

Get Claude Code

Anthropic's AI agent that runs in your terminal. If you already have it, skip to step 2.

$ npm install -g @anthropic-ai/claude-code

Requires Node.js 18+ and an Anthropic account.

2

Install the plugin

One command. Open source. Adds two slash commands to your Claude Code.

$ claude plugin install asaferdman23/career-brand-plugin
3

Run /career-brand:brand and paste your details

Copy-paste your LinkedIn about section, resume bullet points, or just type your experience. It builds your profile in 60 seconds, then you're ready to create content.

> /career-brand:brand I just shipped a feature that uses AI to plan events
